Lasagna

lasagna recipe

A dish made up of noodles,cheese and spices

Ingredients

  • 16 ounce package noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 16 ounce jar of spagehtti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozarella cheese
  • 1/2 pounded shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 pound ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Bring pot of salted water to boil and add noodles when done drain
  2. Brown beef in a skillet and season with salt and pepper
  3. Combine cheese and put in a pan,alternate layers noodles,meat and cheese
  4. Bake in a preheated oven
