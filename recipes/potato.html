Mashed Potato recipe

A dish made up of potatoes ,cream,milk and butter

Ingredients

  • 1 pound potatoes
  • 2 cubes chicken bullion
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter/li>
  • 2 tablespoons milk

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes and add to pressure cooker with water and cubes,set timer for high
  2. Mix potatoes and water,allow for 15mins for pressure to build
  3. Release pressure and drain the liquid
  4. Add sour cream,butter and milk,gently mash potatoes with potato masher
