Pancake recipe

A dish made up flour,milk and sugar

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 teaspoon baking powder
  • Salt and sugar to taste
  • 16 ounce jar of spagehtti sauce
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 3 table spoon butter
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Sift flour,baking powder,salt and sugar.Make a well in the center and pour milk,egg and butter mix till smooth
  2. Ligthly oil pan on medium heat,scoop batter in pan and brown both sides
